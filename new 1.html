I am Udayanga Srimal, originally from Mahiyangana, Sri Lanka. Currently, I am a dedicated student at the University of Sri Jayawardenepura, where I am pursuing a degree in Software Engineering. Alongside my academic pursuits, I am deeply passionate about entrepreneurship. I established a business venture called "Us Chocolate Bravo," showcasing my entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to innovation. My interests extend beyond photography; I possess a keen eye for design and enjoy the creative process of editing and crafting visual content.
